⚠️ Important Context

🎯 Educational Purpose

This tool demonstrates algorithm behavior, not content recommendations. Items are labeled abstractly (Item_001, Item_002) to focus attention on collaborative filtering patterns rather than content similarity.

📊 Research Focus

Results reflect performance on a specific dataset and should not be generalized to all recommendation scenarios. Different datasets, sparsity levels, and user behaviors may favor different algorithms.

📋 Dataset Characteristics

Size: 1200 users × 1200 items
Observed Ratings: 1,111,768 out of 1.44M possible
Sparsity: 22.8% (realistic for rec systems)
Rating Scale: 1-5 stars
